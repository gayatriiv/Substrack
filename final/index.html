Key Features

Track All Subscriptions

Add and group subscriptions like streaming, utilities, or magazines. Set payment dates and methods.

Never Miss a Payment

Get reminders via email or app alerts before payments are charged to avoid late fees.

See Where Your Money Goes

View charts showing monthly costs and which subscriptions are your biggest spenders.

Save Money on Unused Subs

Get suggestions for cancelling or downgrading subscriptions you rarely use.

Stay Within Budget

Set monthly spending limits and get alerts when you're about to go over budget.

Shared Plan Contributions

Invite others to join subscription groups and track who has paid their share.

About SubsTrack

SubsTrack was created to help people manage the growing number of subscription services that have become part of everyday life. Our mission is to help you save money and avoid unwanted charges by keeping all your subscriptions organized in one place. We're committed to helping you take control of your recurring payments and make smarter financial decisions.
