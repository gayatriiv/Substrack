Our Values

Our Founder

Gayatri, CEO and Founder of Substrack,is a sophmore CS student with a passion for building innovative solutions. Inspired by her boyfriends' struggles with managing subscriptions she came up with the idea to simplify the process and make his life easier.

Her vision for Substrack is to create a world where managing subscriptions is effortless and transparent.
